Fresh Tomato Sauce Recipe

Description:

This is a recipe for fresh tomato sauce.

This recipe assumes you have a high-sided pan.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Spoonful of minced garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • etc.

Steps:

  1. Add the garlic and any onion or shallot to a warm high-sided pan with olive oil to sautee for 1 minute.
  2. Add tomatoes and mushrooms to the pan and mix thoroughly with spices.
  3. Once the tomatoes are cooked down, add ajvar or any other flavourings along with lightly cooked pasta.
